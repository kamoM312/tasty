The Restaurant

It may sound far fetched, but our esteemed Restaurant franchise began as a small caravan. Our founders, Kabelo and Steven, came together after completing their Hospitality studies. We now bring Italian inspired Pizza to Mzansi.

As crazy as it may sound, the results speak for themselves. Keep scrolling to read the full story.

  • Humble Beginnings

  • Wild Idea (2014)

    Two friends, Kabelo and Steven, came together to start a local Pizza caravan.

  • Local Sensation (2015)

    The Pizza Caravan is a massive hit. Our founders are able to rapidly expand their caravan outlets.


  • The Big Opening

  • Opening The First Restaurant (2018)

    Kaleb and Steven have gathered enough funds and open their first restaurant in the Johannesburg Central Business District.

  • Once in a Lifetime (2021)

    A group of investors approach our founders with a plan to expand the business.

  • A Leap of Faith (2023)

    Our founders take the offer and expand across various cities in Gauteng. It is the birth of a new Franchise!

  • Present Day

    The journey continues...
